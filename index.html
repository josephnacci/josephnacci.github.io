How much more or less do Price Chopper shoppers care about climate change?


The circles on this map correspond to the relative number of Price Chopper shoppers in the north-eastern states. People who primarily do their grocery shopping at Price Chopper tend to be among the more eco-conscious grocery store shoppers in general, but as most of the stores are in states with high sensitivity to climate change, the color of the circles represents the over/under index compared to overall climate sentiment in each state.



What brands to Price Chopper shoppers use most often

The horizontal axis shows the overall fraction of the population that is a current user of a product. The vertical shows how much this more this product is used by Price Chopper shoppers.
